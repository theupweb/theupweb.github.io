The Unpretentious Web.

Community of Web developers
The Unpretentious Web is an initiative to focus on productive skills for web development. Due to amid outbreak of COVID-19, most of us are sitting at home pausing all the outdoor activities. It's the right time to learn, collaborate and contribute. The key aspect of this community is to learn web developement in an effective way, right from the basics. Get access to resources, links, PDFs and chance to make your own projects and collaborate with others.
